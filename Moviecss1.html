Fast and Furious

The Fast and the Furious (also known as Fast & Furious) is an American franchise including a series of action films, which center around illegal street racing and heists, and various other media portraying the characters and situations from the films. An elite street racer and ex-convict Dominic Toretto and his crew: Jesse , Leon , Vince and Letty Ortiz ,are under suspicion of stealing expensive electronic equipment by hijacking moving trucks, Brian O'Conner is an undercover police officer who attempts to find out who exactly is stealing the equipment. He works for FBI agent Bilkins and LAPD Sgt. Tanner.

Roles and Actors

  • Dominic Toretto - Vin Diesel
  • Jesse – Chad Lindberg
  • Leon – Johnny Strong
  • Vince – Matt Schulze
  • Letty Ortiz – Michelle Rodriguez
  • Brian O'Conner – Paul Walker
  • FBI agent Bilkins – Thom Barry
  • LAPD Sgt. Tanner – Ted Levine
