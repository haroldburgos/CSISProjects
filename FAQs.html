Where can I get my dog vaccininated?

You could go to any vet clinic or animal hospitals to get your dogs vacciniated ,but
convientely Kansas City's Spay and Nuetr provide the same vaccines with cheaper packages
and friendly staff.


When should I start potty training?

With Puppies, start taking them out first thing in the morning. After that take them out every 30 minutes to one hour.
Since puppies do not have much control over their blatter make sure to begin and schedule and always keep a look out for messes
It would also be best practices to put them down at the same spot when taking out to go do thier business.
For older dogs having a constant schedule would help build them build a scheduled bladder so that when you come home, you can
not expect much of a mess


What type of food should I give my dog?

The most popular brand of food for puppies would be puppy chow. I would try by choosing one type of your choice
and see how your puppy reacts if he or she does not like it then try another brand. It might take a few tries
Before you find the right one.
For older dogs I would recommend Dog Chow: Light and Healthy, to maintain a healthy weight and happy dog.
I feed casey this brand and she tends to love it when ever she notices the purple bag.
Note: It is recommended to not change thier type of food to often as it can cause you dog to feel ill, if you are
transitioning food types, try having a slow transition over time before completely serving a new brand.


How much interaction does my dog need?

This depends on the type and also age of the dog. For younger puppies they are a fur ball of energy and need constant
attention and interaction. As dogs get older they can either maintain the same energy or drop a few levels.
It is very important that whatever type of dog you are making sure it is getting its nessesary exercise.
This will decrease their energy to rest for the next day and reduce thier anxiety to run around the house trying to
burn some energy.

Should I adopt or purchase from a breeder?

The Prefered method would be adoption. Most dogs at shelters are in line ready to be put down because not thier being adopted
in time. Due to an over population is dogs it would be both cost effiecent and helpful to adopt a dog from a local shelter
Most dogs come from a bad home and need a second chance to demostrate that they too can be loved.
