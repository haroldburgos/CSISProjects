Mini Deep Dish Pizza

Mini Deep Dish Pizza speaks for it self. It is a quick and easy deep dish pizza platter that can be used as a snack for any family and friends event. This dish should only take about 20 minutes to make and should be little to no mess at all. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Marinara Sauce - 1.5 cups
  • Parmedan Cheese – 1/3 cups
  • Pepper – 1/2 tsp
  • Salt – 1/2 tsp
  • Red Pepper Flakes – 1 tsp
  • Oregano – 1/2 tsp
  • 12in Torilla (Optional Replacement: Bread) – 1 tortilla (quantity depends on party size )
  • Mozzarella Cheese – 1 bag
  • Mini Pepperoni – 1 bag
  • Cupcake Pan – 1 pan
  • Pan Spray - 1 bottle

Preparation Steps

  1. Start by mixing all of the spices mentioned above with the Marinara Sauce. Mix until all of the spices and sauce are blended and thick.
  2. Cut the torilla into small circles similar to the size of the cupcake pan circles.
  3. Spray the pan with pan grease spray
  4. Then place the small cut tortilla circles inside of the cupcake pans and fill them in with the marinara sauce.
  5. Top the mini pizzas with mozzarella cheese and mini pepperoni slices to each one.
  6. After place them in the oven at 425 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes
  7. Serve them on a plate and enjoy
